Scoring in Mah-Jongg

平和
One Point

All Runs (平和) - no triples, no winds or dragons as pair




All Simples (斷么) - no ones, nines or winds or dragons




Two Suits (缺一門) - no winds or dragons, lacking one suit




Middle Pair (將) - hand has pair of twos, fives or eights




Duplicate Runs (一般高) - two runs of the same suit and same rank.




Two Concealed Triples (兩暗嵌) - two triples in hand




Young and Old (老少) - 123-789 of the same suit




Four of a Kind Split (四歸一) - four identical tiles spread over 2 clusters




Two Winds (兩風) - two triples of winds




Three Fives (三五) - hand contains three fives of different suits




Two-Suit Dragon (二色龍) - pure dragon but in two suits




Fully Concealed (門清) - no clusters laid out, wins on discard

Self-Draw (自摸) - draw winning tile

Kong Blossom (槓上花) - self draw on melded kong

Kong Discard Win (槓上砲) - wins when someone discards melded kong

Robbing the Kong (搶槓) - winning off the discard of a melded kong

Last Tile Draw (海底撈) - self draw on last piece

Last Discard Win (海底砲) - wins on last discard

Blocking Win (攔和) - block someone else's win

Last Available Tile (絕張) - wins on a piece, other three of which are discarded

Single Wait (獨聽) - waiting for only one tile

No Fives (缺五) - no fives

Winning Five (嵌心五) - wins on five with single wait

Four Melds at Once (清四碰) - pong four times, last pong occurs at win, all triples is immediate

Two Pairs (對碰) - two pairs before win, other tiles arbitrary

Two points

Terminal Triples (老少碰) - triples of ones and nines in the same suit




Same Rank Sets (帶尾) - two triples and pair of same rank, or three consecutive ranks of same suit in two triples and pair







Dragon Pair (一元半) - dragons in a triple and in pair




Double Last Tiles (雙絕) - waiting for two pieces, seven of which are discarded

Half Dependent (半求人) - four clusters laid out, pair in hand, must be self draw

Three points

Two Winds Plus Pair (兩風半) - winds in two triples and in pair




Four in Three Sets (四歸二) - four identical tiles spread over 3 clusters




Fully Dependent (全求人) - all five clusters obtained from others

Double Kong (兩槓) - two kongs

Concealed Self-Draw (門清不求) - no clusters out and self draw

Four points

Pure Dragon (一條龍) - all ranks of one suit represented in three runs




Sister Flowers (姐妹花) - three runs of the same rank in three different suits




All Triples (對對和) - all triples




Half Flush (混一色) - missing two suits, winds or dragons permitted




All Five Sets (五門齊) - one cluster for each house




Mixed Terminals (混帶么) - ones, nines and winds or dragons in every cluster




Pure Terminals (清帶么) - ones and nines in every cluster




Three Concealed Triples (三暗嵌) - three triples in hand




Five Points

Two Dragons (二元) - dragons in two triples




Triple Ladder (節節高) - three consecutive runs of same suit




Six Points

Double Dragon (雙條龍) - pure dragon with duplicate run




Double Sister Flowers (雙姐妹花) - Sister Flowers with duplicate run




Triple Run Ladder (三般高) - three consecutive triples of same suit




Double Four Split (雙四歸一) - two Four of a Kind Split spread over 9 tiles



Eight Points

Three Winds (三風碰) - winds in three triples




Sister Triples (姐妹碰) - three triples of same rank, or three triples of same suit in consecutive rank







Extended Four Split (雙四歸一) - two Four of a Kind Split spread over 12 tiles




Triple Kong (三槓) - three kongs

Ten Points

Double Young-Old (雙標) - two young-old




Little Three Dragons (小三元) - dragons in two triples and pair




Double Same Rank (雙帶尾) - two same rank sets




Fifteen Points

Three Ranks Only (三數) - only three ranks used, winds count as one rank, as do dragons







Triple Four Split (三個四歸一) - three Four of a Kind Split




All One Rank (全帶) - single rank present in all clusters




Double Dragon Hug (雙龍抱) - two duplicate runs




Sister Kongs (姐妹槓) - sister triples with kongs

Eighteen points

Three Sequential Ranks (連三數) - three ranks only with three consecutive ranks, no winds or dragons




Twenty Points

Pure Flush (清一色) - everything in one suit




All Honors (全字碰) - all winds or dragons




Mixed Terminal Triples (混么碰) - only ones, nines and winds or dragons in every cluster




Twenty-four points

Four Concealed Triples (四暗嵌) - four triples in hand




Four in Four Sets (四歸四) - all four of one tile spread over four clusters




Twenty-eight points

Big Three Dragons (大三元) - three triples of dragon




Double Terminal Triples (雙老少碰) - two sets of terminal triples




Thirty points

Thirteen Terminals (十三么) - all ones, nines and winds or dragons




Forty points

Two Ranks Only (二數) - only two ranks used, winds counting as one rank, as do dragons




All Nines (全帶九) - nines appear in all clusters




All Middles (將將碰) - clusters contains only twos, fives and eights




Little Four Winds (小四喜) - 3 clusters of winds, pair of winds




Double Four in Four (雙四歸四) - all four of two tiles spread over four clusters




Fifty Points

Big Four Winds (大四喜) - four clusters of winds, pair not needed




All Honors (全字碰) - all winds or dragons




Double Terminal Special (雙老少碰) - swang-terminal triples and two ranks only




Twelve Maidens (十二金釵) - four consecutive triples of same rank




Sixty Points

Four Identical Runs (三個四歸四) - four identical runs




Heavenly Hand (天和) - dealer wins immediately

Earthly Hand (地和) - wins on first draw

Human Hand (人和) - wins on first discard

Eighteen Arhats (十八羅漢) - four kongs, pair not needed
